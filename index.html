RWAs Explained
in 5 minutes.

One idea at a time. Press play and let it guide you — or skip around. No jargon, no homework.

CHAPTER 01 · WALLETS~1 MIN

One app for everything you own.

Money used to live in two places — a bank account for cash, a broker account for investments. A wallet replaces both. It holds your crypto and your RWAs side by side, and most let you buy stablecoins right inside. Minutes to start, no paperwork.

CHAPTER 02 · STABLECOINS~1 MIN

Digital dollars, always on.

A stablecoin is worth $1 and moves anywhere on Earth in seconds — nights, weekends and holidays included. Most wallet providers offer them built in, so they're usually the first RWA you'll ever hold.

CHAPTER 03 · RWA VAULTS~1 MIN

Your stablecoins, put to work.

Vaults let you easily deposit stablecoins into ready-made strategies backed by different real assets — government bonds, credit, property. The yield arrives onchain, in plain sight, every day.

CHAPTER 04 · TRADING & AMM~1 MIN

Markets that never close.

Tokenized assets trade around the clock on automated markets — no closing bell, no middleman. When you want out, you can pull your asset faster, typically at any time — though fees always apply.

CHAPTER 05 · NO-MIDDLEMAN LOANS~1 MIN

Borrow or lend in minutes.

Put up a tokenized asset and borrow against it instantly — or lend your stablecoins and start earning interest from day one. Straight from your wallet: no bank approval, no paperwork, no waiting.
